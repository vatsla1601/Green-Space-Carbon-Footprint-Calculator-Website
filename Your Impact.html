  • Lifestyle
    1. Plant-based diets
      1. Pushing to implement more greens in your meals
      2. As it takes 5,000 gallons of water to produce 1 pound of beef
    2. Education Around E-Waste
      1. There is a major issues with how much electronic waste there is in the world

      2. Using compostable products and using waste as compost
    4. Raising Awareness of Environmental Issues
      1. Educating and understanding the issues that affect the world
      2. Spreading awareness about the electronic devices that release CO2
    5. Being More Mindful
      1. Understanding what you are consuming and its value
      2. Being cautious of fuel consumption
      3. Switching to Green Energy when possible
    6. Second Hand Clothing
      1. Less consumption and promotion of fast fashion
      2. Reusing clothes through families as applicable as to not over purchase as children grow quickly and clothes can be repurposed
      3. Thrifting clothes when able to, as a way to reduce waste and reuse items that are not yet ready to be thrown away
